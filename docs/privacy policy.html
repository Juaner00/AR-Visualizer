Effective date: 10/1/2025

This Privacy Policy explains how SERIINCO ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and discloses information when you use the APP SERIINCO mobile application.

1. Information We Access

Camera access: The app requests the device camera permission (android.permission.CAMERA) to provide in-app features that require a live camera feed (for example, augmented reality or photo capture).

We do not access any other personal data unless explicitly requested and disclosed in this policy or in-app prompts.

2. How We Use the Camera

  • The camera feed is used only in real-time inside the app to support features described in the app interface.

  • If you save or share media, those files are handled according to the app's UI choices and any additional permissions you grant.

3. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We do not sell or share personal data collected through the camera with third parties for their own purposes. If the app integrates third-party SDKs that require camera access, we will disclose that in-app or in this policy and those SDKs will be governed by their own privacy policies.

4. Data Retention & Security

If the app stores any images or media locally (because you explicitly saved them), those files remain on your device and are controlled by your device’s file-management and privacy settings. We take reasonable measures to protect data, but no method of transmission or electronic storage is 100% secure.

5. Children

Our app is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, contact us and we will take steps to delete such information.
